Projects

Throughout my years of being a Developer, I have worked on various projects. Some have been really interesting and I enjoyed overcoming the task at hand.

Logging into HMRC (Casper JS)

A lot of the information at Intouch came directly from HMRC. In some cases this meant immitating a login to their website, and collecting the information on the page.
Our team found that Casper JS was a good solution to our problem. Through Javascript, it logged into the page, and stored the content of the URL we wanted in a file. We could then scrape through the file and extract the information we were interested in.

Contractor UK Award (CURL)

Contractor UK did a yearly vote on a selection of categories. We knew we were capable of winning, we just needing to make voting as easy as possible for our clients. I designed and implemented an easy 2 button page, one to vote and one to skip voting.
The problem we faced, was that the Contactor UK voting process used a catpcha. To counteract this, we used CURL to return the page HTML and then searched it for an image with the title "Image CAPTCHA". This then enabled us to prompt the user for the captcha value and send it in the response via the Contractor UK API, validating the submission.
